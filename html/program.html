Program

A mixer will be held concurrently with the poster session on Saturday, April 22, from 5:55pm–6:30pm. The conference dinner will follow at 7:00pm at Sichuan Pepper, in Vernon.

Lunch and the closing Travelers Reception are included in registration. The dinner has limited space. Please register early and indicate whether you will attend.
